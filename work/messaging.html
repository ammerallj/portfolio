Creating Continuity Across Messaging

Conversations that don't leave, as messaging expands across the app.

Timeline
July–November 2023
Role
Design Lead · Interaction Design · Product Strategy
Team
Engineering · Content Design · Research · Data Science

On Facebook, private sharing is how conversations start—a Reel or listing sent to one person—but continuing meant replying in Messenger. As messaging expanded, entry points designed in isolation made conversations less predictable.

This work shipped to millions of people across Facebook but the full case study is confidential.

View case study (opens in new tab)

Need access? Request the password at ammerallj@gmail.com

Impact

  • Standardized in-app messaging across Facebook surfaces by designing a reusable interaction model that continued guiding rollout after handoff.
  • Informed a rollout addressing ~144M monthly switches from Facebook to Messenger, advancing Facebook's strategy to bring private sharing back into the core app.
  • Validated a key part of the interaction model through experimentation: +21.9% thread opens on iOS and +27.1% on Android, while maintaining neutral topline metrics.
