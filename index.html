Career Story

Over the past nine years at Microsoft and Meta, I've helped products evolve alongside changing technologies, teams, and user needs.

Looking back, I've always been drawn to the work beneath the interface—creating the foundations that help products stay coherent as they evolve.

Outside of Work

I spend my time chasing after my dachshund, Maeve, and serving on the board of my condominium association, where I enjoy solving the long-term challenges of a small community.

Much of my creative energy comes from discovering new music and exploring art exhibitions. I love collecting ideas, moods, and perspectives that often become the backdrop for whatever season of life I'm in.
